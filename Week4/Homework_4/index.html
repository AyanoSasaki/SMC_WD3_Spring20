Location and Parking

Located in the Mojave Desert 25 miles south of Las Vegas on the Jean Dry Lake Bed. Parking is available on Jean Dry Lake Bed with purchase of a $29 parking pass.

If approaching from the south on I-15 , take exit 12 for Jean Goodsprings and follow signage and traffic management to Jean Dry Lake Bed. If approaching from the North on I-15, take exit 25 for Sloan and follow signage and traffic management to the venue.

Parking hang tags will be mailed to the address you provide when purchasing your ticket. They must be visible at the time you arrive at the venue.

Parking for RV’s is not allowed.

Please note that due to the remote location of the event, Ubers, Lyfts, and rideshares of any type are not allowed.

Walk

There is a 10-15 minute 1000 meter walk from the parking/shuttle area to the venue. Plan accordingly and wear comfortable footwear.

Last updated 2 hrs ago

ADA Accommodations

If you are unable to make the 1000 meter walk from the parking / shuttle area to the venue, we provide a complementary shuttle for those with ADA needs. This shuttle will run from the parking / shuttle area to the venue.

If you plan to make use of this shuttle, please indicate that you have ADA needs when you purchase tickets. You will receive two complimentary tickets for the on-site ADA shuttle, one for you and one for a guest to accompany you.

If you are in need of a wheel chair lift from either the strip or the on site ADA shuttle, please provide that information through the ticketing process.
